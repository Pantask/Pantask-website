How soon can we get started?

We will try to have your VA ready for you in 48 hours or less. More specialized VAs such as graphic designers can take a little longer.

Do you have a money back guarantee?

We offer the first five hours of work with a money back guarantee, so you can easily discover for yourself is the quality of service meets your expectations.

How many hours do you recommend to start?

If you are a small startup or entrepreneur, we recommend securing a PA for 5-10 hours a week to start with.

What can I use Pantask for?

We'll take on pretty much any tasks you have. We also offer graphic design and copyediting services.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! If you are a startup or an entrepreneur you may be entitled to a discount for your first month with Pantask.

Where can I contact you?

Send us an email at ask@pantask.com, or call us at (570) 509-3366 to schedule a meeting. We'll get back to you within 24 hours or less.

When do I pay?

We invoice on a monthly basis.

Do you accept payment in cryptocurrency?

We are currently looking into accepting payment in several cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and ADA. Let us know if this option interests you.
